COVID-19 Time Series Data

This image is a choropleth map of Australia, displaying COVID-19 confirmed case data by state between January 22, 2020 and March 8, 2024. The map is color-coded to represent the number of confirmed cases, with darker shades indicating higher numbers. Key details from the map:

  • New South Wales is highlighted with the darkest shade, indicating the highest number of confirmed cases, totaling 3,908,129.
  • Other states and territories are colored in varying lighter shades, signifying lower confirmed case counts relative to New South Wales.
  • The map includes a dropdown menu for selecting different variables (Confirmed, Recovered and Deaths) and a date slider at the bottom for selecting specific dates within the time series data.
